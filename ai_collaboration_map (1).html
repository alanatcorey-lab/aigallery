When AI enters a workflow, the question is not whether humans are still needed, it is where human judgment is irreplaceable. This map traces two real-world workflows from a partner organization, showing exactly where AI takes the lead, where humans must step in, and why the handoff points matter for accountability, ethics, and trust.

Core principle AI handles speed and scale. Humans handle judgment and accountability.
